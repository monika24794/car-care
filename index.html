Car Polishing

About Us

CarKeepers offers doorstep car cleaning services, car wash, car detailing services. Our aim is to provide the best doorstep car cleaning and polishing services with a fully equipped team. We have introduced a concept of doorstep car detailing. Our fully equipped team comes to your convenient place (Home/office) where we serve you the best in class car detailing service.

Why did we start up- Car is an important part of lifestyle. We, with family and friends, spend hours in the small cabin of a car. While every car owner has an intention to keep his car clean and bacteria free, it's very difficult to take time. Here we, team CarKeepers come to help you to maintain your car. Our well trained team comes with fully equipped vans and machines like steam cleaners, upholstery machines, vacuum cleaners, pressure pumps, foam gun, dual action polisher etc.


There we serve you the car wash, car interior cleaning, antibacterial car cleaning, car steam cleaning services followed by car polishing, ceramic coating, car detailing services, Teflon coating services etc.

As our set up is mobile, you don’t need to come to our office/store and spend extra time travelling. Instead we come to your place for car cleaning service. Car interior cleaning is as easy as ever before.

Why Choose Us- We are honest about our work in order to be fair and ethical. We use carefully selected car detailing products & tools sourced from world’s best companies. Our vision is to be the most trusted, recognized and respected car cleaning service provider in pune. We develop a long term relationship with our customers by practicing fair principles.
